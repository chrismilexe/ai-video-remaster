## Video Description

What if one of the rarest forms of strength is not knowing how to push, but knowing how to stop?

In this episode of The Outcome Code, we break down why restraint is a form of intelligence, why continuation can become a trap, and how to recognize the last clean moment before effort starts costing more than it gives.

This is not anti-ambition content.
It is a practical system for reading limits, pressure, timing, and self-control.

Topics in this episode:
- why stopping is not weakness
- when persistence turns into damage
- how momentum traps people
- why restraint protects judgment
- four questions to ask before continuing
