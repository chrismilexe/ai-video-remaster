## Video Description

What if anxiety is not always a sign of danger, but a sign that something is out of rhythm?

In this episode of The Outcome Code, we break down why anxiety can signal misalignment, why speed and pressure can quietly distort judgment, and how to read overwhelm more accurately instead of just trying to suppress it.

This is not therapy content.
It is a practical system for reading pace, pressure, timing, and internal signals.

Topics in this episode:
- why anxiety is not always danger
- how pace can create psychological strain
- why rest is not the same as collapse
- how modern life rewards misalignment
- better questions to ask when anxiety rises
