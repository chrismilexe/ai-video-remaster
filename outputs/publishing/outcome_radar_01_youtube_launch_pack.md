## Video Description

In this Outcome Radar episode, we break down a current U.S.-China tariff confrontation and the deeper pattern behind it:
what happens when pressure stops producing compliance and starts producing retaliation instead.

This is not a trade-policy explainer.
It is a pattern read on escalation, counterpressure, face-saving exits, and why force often turns expensive once both sides start defending identity.

Topics in this episode:
- when pressure creates counterpressure
- why escalation can harden the other side
- the importance of calibration
- how retaliation becomes easier to predict
