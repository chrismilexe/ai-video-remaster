## Video Description

In this Outcome Radar episode, we use Tesla's latest sales story to examine a bigger pattern:
what happens when a founder's identity becomes so concentrated that every public conflict starts affecting the business itself.

This is not just a story about EV demand.
It is a pattern read on trust, exposure, founder intensity, and the cost of letting the person overwhelm the product.

Topics in this episode:
- why attention is not the same as trust
- when founder energy starts narrowing the brand
- how consumer businesses pay for public identity
- the hidden cost of concentrated leadership
